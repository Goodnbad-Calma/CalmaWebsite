CEO’s Message

Where 2,000 dreams find their address.

Dear Valued Partners and Future Residents,


When I founded CALMA, I envisioned more than just a real estate development company. I saw an opportunity to redefine what it means to create spaces that truly matter – spaces where vision takes shape and where potential flourishes.

Today, as I reflect on our journey of 28 successfully delivered projects across 3 cities, I'm reminded that our greatest achievement isn't measured in square meters or construction milestones. It's measured in the 2,000+ families who now call our developments home, and in the communities we've nurtured that transcend borders while honoring local traditions.

At CALMA, our foundation is built on unwavering reliability and proven credibility. Every project, every deadline, every promise – delivered with precision and pride. This commitment to excellence has earned us the trust of people across Saudi Arabia, and it drives everything we do as we expand our footprint across the Kingdom.

Our approach goes beyond traditional construction. We're architecting the future of urban living, integrating cutting-edge sustainability practices that align with Saudi Vision 2030's environmental goals. From Riyadh to the wider Saudi Arabia, we're building spaces where global citizens feel at home, creating inclusive environments where families thrive and businesses prosper.

As we look toward the future, CALMA represents the pinnacle of real estate excellence, where visionary development meets uncompromising standards. We're not just raising skylines – we're elevating lifestyles and setting new benchmarks in luxury development that inspire and endure.

This is more than real estate; this is the future of refined living. This is CALMA – committed to your calm.

Musab Al-Majed

Chief Executive Officer | CALMA
